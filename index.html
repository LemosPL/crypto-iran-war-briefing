The Iran war is not random in this lens — it is part of the established order's last attempt to maintain control before decentralized systems render that control structurally impossible. USD 5T Liquidity

The conflict could paradoxically accelerate the very liquidity conditions that validate the bull case — if it stays contained. Pre-war quote: "Any liquidity crisis will be swiftly dealt with by the Fed."
Key Risk:
Prolonged
Conflict		 Moss has consistently stated that "how much carnage between here and there" is the real variable. If Hormuz stays closed, oil stays elevated, inflation re-accelerates, and the Fed is trapped between growth and price stability — that is the bear scenario. His consistent advice: "Do not buy anything you would not hold for 10 years." The structural Bitcoin thesis remains intact regardless of outcome. The question is only the path and how much volatility investors must endure to reach the destination.
